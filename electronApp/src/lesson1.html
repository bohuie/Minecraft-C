Lesson 1:Gold Miner

Welcome to the gold miner lesson. In this lesson you will program your agent to collect gold blocks for you.


First, click 'import' to import world of 'Gold Miner' to your game.



Then, in the game press 'c' on your keyboard to open the code builder in game press this button for the lesson.



These are the functions that you will need for your lesson, try them in the game to see what happens!



Information   for   This   Lesson


Background:

Finding specific resources in Minecraft has always been a cumbersome and time-consuming task, but now you can program your agent to help you accomplish this task quickly. In this lesson we have set up three mines, each with different lengths, widths and depths(There is no more gold after a specific depth). You can choose any of them to test your code.
